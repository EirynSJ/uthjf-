The Presence of God

As our savior, God saves us in ways we sometimes don’t realize. Even the slightest, simplest ways, like creating the existence of natural resources, are essential for us human beings. The natural world is seen as part of God's creation, which He entrusted humanity to care for. The beauty and functionality of a healthy environment reflect God's love and generosity.

A healthy environment supports physical and mental well-being, which aligns with Jesus’ emphasis on healing and providing for human needs.

God also thought ahead on creating us human beings on the sixth day after he created the whole world, showing that he cares and knows that without all the things he created for us, we wouldn’t be able to survive. He saved us, knowing we wouldn’t survive with such nothingness, and created such a beautiful world for us from the start giving us all the resources and healthy environment.
