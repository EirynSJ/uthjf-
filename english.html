Why Surabaya is the Cleanest City in Indonesia

Surabaya has been recognized with the prestigious Adipura Kencana trophy, reflecting its dedication to keeping the environment pristine and sustainable. As the capital of East Java, this city takes great pride in its cleanliness and eco-friendly practices. Surabaya promotes Eco-tourism, encouraging nature-friendly activities such as kayaking, nature treks, and city tours that showcase the vast green parks and animal conservation areas.

The city's eco-conscious accommodations are also a highlight, offering visitors the chance to minimize their environmental impact while enjoying locally sourced organic food, rainwater harvesting, and efficient waste reduction strategies.

One of Surabaya's standout initiatives is its advanced waste management system, which categorizes waste into organic, inorganic, and hazardous materials for easier recycling. The "waste bank" system allows residents to deposit waste in exchange for money, promoting both environmental and economic sustainability.

Green spaces are abundant in Surabaya, such as the beautiful Surabaya City Forest, which improves air quality. The city also invests heavily in air pollution tracking, ensuring that data-driven policies are enacted to protect the environment and the health of its citizens.

Ultimately, Surabaya is a beautiful, sustainable destination. Visitors not only get to experience the rich culture of East Java but also breathe cleaner air while learning how small changes can make a big difference to the environment. Surabaya invites you to come and experience its green transformation firsthand, where both nature and people thrive in harmony.
